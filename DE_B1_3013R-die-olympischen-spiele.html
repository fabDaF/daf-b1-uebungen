🇬🇧 Background in English:
The Olympic Games are the world's biggest sporting event, held every four years. Athletes from around the world compete in many sports. The Games began in ancient Greece and were revived in 1896 in Athens. The motto is "Citius, Altius, Fortius" — Faster, Higher, Stronger.
