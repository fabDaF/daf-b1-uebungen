🇬🇧 Background in English:
German literature has produced many world-famous authors. Writers like Goethe, Schiller, Kafka, and Brecht have influenced literature worldwide. Their works explore themes of identity, society, and human nature. Many have received international awards and their works have been translated into dozens of languages.
